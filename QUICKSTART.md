   The app will automatically open at `http://localhost:3000`

## First Time Setup

1. When you first open the app, you'll see the **Cycle Setup** page
2. Enter your:
   - Last period start date (required)
   - Average cycle length (default: 28 days)
   - Average period duration (default: 5 days)
   - Optional symptoms (cramps, fatigue, anxiety, headaches)

## Using the App

### Dashboard
- View your current cycle phase
- See daily mood, energy, and focus predictions
- Get phase-specific wellness advice
- Receive gentle affirmations on low-mood days

### Schedule
- Add tasks with priorities (high, medium, low)
- Get smart recommendations based on your energy levels
- See which tasks are best for today

### Games
- **Breathing Exercise**: Follow animated sakura petals for guided breathing
- **Memory Game**: Match sakura-themed cards to improve focus

### Companion
- Chat with your AI companion
- Get supportive, empathetic responses
- Receive cycle-aware advice and encouragement
